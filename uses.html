Uses of Synthetic Protein Design

AI Synthetic protein design could enable progress in all sorts of areas.

Environment

We could use enzymes to breakdown plastic waste or even to capture carbon from the atmosphere.

Imagine a future where we can understand disease more quickly and develop drugs to fight them.

It could also be used to help plants grow in a changing climate.

Medicine

It could transform our treatment of a range of diseases.

Imagine a future where we can understand disease more quickly and develop drugs to fight them.

It could help us produce cheap and effective vaccines.
